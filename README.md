Inspired by virtual keyboards in games and other operating systems, ConType brings that experience to the desktop, specifically for Mac.
Made with Swift and SwiftUI, designed to feel like a part of your Mac.

## Features
- **Virtual keyboard**
  <br>Shiny and neatly resembles a real keyboard (Roughly based on the Magic Keyboard)
- **Activate with a shortcut**
  <br>Easily bring up the keyboard from your controller
- **Type faster with hotkeys**
  <br>Easily access common actions like space, and shift with your controller buttons
